# AI Content Generator 🚀

An intelligent, all-in-one platform designed for students and creators to streamline their content workflow using Artificial Intelligence.

## ✨ Key Features

* **Role-Based Content:** Tailored output by selecting your persona (e.g., Student, Founder).
* **Smart Analysis:** Paste any URL for the AI to analyze and extract key insights.
* **Asset Management:** Upload images or files to include in your content generation process.
* **History & Feedback:** Keep track of your previous generated content and provide feedback to improve future results.

## 🛠 Tech Stack

* **Framework:** Streamlit
* **AI Engine:** Generative AI
* **Platform:** Python

## 💡 Why this tool?

This tool eliminates the hassle of switching between multiple platforms. It is built for efficiency, allowing you to go from research to final draft in just a few clicks.
